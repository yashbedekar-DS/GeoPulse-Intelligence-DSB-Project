Reading the data

Silver peaked first (Jan 2026) on industrial demand — solar, EVs, defence electronics. Gold peaked a month later (Feb 2026), coinciding with the Iran–Hormuz strikes, consistent with safe-haven flows. Brent's move was the sharpest and most event-tied: +52% in a single month once the Strait of Hormuz was disrupted. Event links here rest on timing — association, not proven causation.
